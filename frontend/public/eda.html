Seasonal Pattern Confirmed
Groundwater worst in Q2 (Apr–Jun) before monsoon. Q3 (Aug) shows 1.5–3 mbgl recovery from monsoon recharge. Net annual depletion: ~0.8 mbgl/yr in stressed states.
Top Depletion Drivers
Irrigation well density (r=0.78), agricultural area (r=0.65), and cumulative rainfall deficit (r=0.71) are the strongest predictors of critical depletion risk.
Rajasthan & Punjab Alert
These 2 states account for 58% of critical districts. Punjab's Green Revolution irrigation patterns have caused 15m+ depletion in some districts since 2000.
